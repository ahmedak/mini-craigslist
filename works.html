How mini-craigslist works

Craigslist allows people to post and view ads about virtually anything.
Mini-craigslist aims to do the same thing, except things are, well 'mini'.

So we have three broad categories: Jobs, Services and Goods.
Users can login and post ads in any of these three categories. Others can then view their ad and get contact details of the owner.





So take a look around and see if you find anything interesting to buy!
